This page walks through the process of creating your Game Warden account, from initial registration through MFA enrollment and organization linking. Complete these steps before attempting to submit an application.

## Prerequisites

Before creating your account, ensure you have:
- A valid work email address (government contractor or company domain)
- Access to an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator) or a FIDO2 hardware key
- Your organization's information ready (CAGE code, legal name, POC details)

<Callout type="note">
  Use a government-issued or contractor organization email address. Personal email domains (gmail.com, yahoo.com, icloud.com, etc.) are not accepted. If you are a DoD contractor, use your company's registered domain, not a .mil address unless you are a government employee.
</Callout>

## Registration Steps

<Steps>
  <Step step="1" title="Navigate to the Game Warden Portal">
    Open your browser and go to [portal.gamewarden.io](https://portal.gamewarden.io). Click the **Sign Up** button on the landing page.
  </Step>
  <Step step="2" title="Enter Your Email Address">
    Enter your organizational email address and click **Continue**. Game Warden will check that the domain is eligible and send a verification email.
  </Step>
  <Step step="3" title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for an email from `noreply@gamewarden.io` with the subject "Verify your Game Warden account." Click the **Verify Email** button. The link is valid for 24 hours. If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.
  </Step>
  <Step step="4" title="Set Your Password">
    After email verification, you will be prompted to set a password. Passwords must be at least 16 characters and must not be a previously breached password (checked against HaveIBeenPwned). Passphrases are recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step step="5" title="Enroll in Multi-Factor Authentication">
    MFA enrollment is required before you can access any part of the portal. Choose one of:

    - **Authenticator App (TOTP):** Scan the QR code with your authenticator app. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm enrollment.
    - **Hardware Security Key (FIDO2):** Insert your key and follow the browser prompt to register it.

    SMS-based MFA is not available on Game Warden.
  </Step>
  <Step step="6" title="Link to Your Organization">
    After MFA enrollment, you will be asked to link to an organization:

    - **New organization:** Select **Create Organization** and enter your organization details. See [Account Setup](/getting-started/fedramp-account-setup) for required fields.
    - **Existing organization:** Select **Request to Join** and search for your organization by name or CAGE code. Your Org Admin will receive a notification to approve your request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Org Admin Approval

If you are joining an existing organization, your access is not active until an Org Admin approves your request. Org Admins receive an email notification and can approve requests from **Organization Settings > Members > Pending Requests**.

If your Org Admin is unavailable, contact your Second Front account representative who can facilitate approval.

## After Account Creation

Once your account is active:
1. Select your deployment path (DoW, FedRAMP, or Commercial) in the portal. This step provisions your workspace.
2. Review the [Onboarding Process](/getting-started/onboarding) to understand what comes next.
3. Bookmark the portal URL and confirm your MFA backup codes are stored securely.

<Callout type="tip">
  Store your MFA backup codes in a secure password manager. If you lose access to your MFA device and do not have backup codes, account recovery requires identity verification with Second Front support and may take 1–3 business days.
</Callout>
