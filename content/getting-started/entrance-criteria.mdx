Before Game Warden accepts your application for onboarding, it must meet a set of entrance criteria that ensure the application is ready for a secure, production-grade deployment environment. Entrance criteria exist to protect the integrity of the platform and the other tenants sharing it, and to ensure that your onboarding experience is smooth.

<Callout type="important">
  Applications that do not meet entrance criteria will not be approved for onboarding. Attempting to proceed without satisfying these requirements will delay your timeline. Review the criteria thoroughly before submitting your application.
</Callout>

## Why Entrance Criteria Exist

Game Warden hosts applications in highly regulated DoD and federal environments. Introducing an application with unresolved security issues, poor operational hygiene, or incompatible architecture could compromise the security posture of the entire platform. Entrance criteria act as a quality gate that ensures every application meets a minimum bar before it enters the environment.

## Categories of Requirements

Entrance criteria are organized into four categories:

### General Requirements

Basic organizational and operational requirements that apply to all vendors regardless of deployment path. These include having a designated point of contact, documented support SLAs, and a defined application boundary.

See [General Requirements](/getting-started/ec-general) for the full list.

### Technical Requirements

Requirements related to how your application is built and packaged. Game Warden is a Kubernetes-based platform, and your application must be containerized and meet specific container configuration standards.

See [Technical Requirements](/getting-started/ec-technical) for the full list.

### Design Patterns

Requirements related to your application's architecture. Some design patterns are not supported on Game Warden (e.g., privileged containers, host networking). This section helps you understand what is and is not supported.

See [Supported Design Patterns](/getting-started/ec-design-patterns) for details.

### Container Standards

Requirements for the base images your application uses. For IL4/IL5 deployments, containers must use Iron Bank-sourced base images.

See [Recommended Containers](/getting-started/ec-containers) for the full list.

## How Entrance Criteria Are Evaluated

When you submit your application in App Central, the Game Warden onboarding team conducts an entrance criteria review. This includes:

1. **Automated checks** — Pipeline scans that verify container configuration, image sources, and basic security controls
2. **Manual review** — The Second Front onboarding team reviews your application submission for completeness and compliance with each criterion
3. **Findings** — Any criteria not met are reported as findings in App Central with remediation guidance

You can track your entrance criteria status in App Central under **Applications > [Your App] > Onboarding Status**.

## Getting Help

If you are unsure whether your application meets a specific criterion, open a support ticket in App Central or contact your Second Front account representative. It is better to ask before submitting than to receive a denial that resets your onboarding timeline.
