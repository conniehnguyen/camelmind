Game Warden's Commercial deployment path provides a secure, monitored cloud environment for vendors who are not yet targeting classified DoD workloads or do not require a formal federal ATO. It is the fastest path to a production deployment on Game Warden-managed infrastructure.

## Who Is Commercial Deployment For?

Commercial deployment is appropriate for:
- Vendors in early-stage government contracting who need a compliant hosting environment but do not yet have an IL4/IL5 requirement
- Vendors who are piloting or demonstrating their application to a government customer before formal contract award
- Vendors operating under commercial contracts with defense primes or subcontractors
- Teams that need a secure, SOC2-compliant environment without the overhead of RMF or FedRAMP processes

## What Commercial Deployment Offers

Game Warden's commercial environment provides many of the same platform capabilities as the DoW and FedRAMP paths, including:

- **Kubernetes-based container orchestration** — deploy containerized workloads with resource isolation and auto-scaling
- **CI/CD pipeline integration** — automated build, scan, and deploy pipeline with vulnerability scanning
- **Harbor container registry** — private registry with image signing and vulnerability reports
- **Managed services** — PostgreSQL, Redis, object storage, secrets management
- **Monitoring and logging** — Grafana/Kibana dashboards, centralized log aggregation
- **SOC2 Type II and ISO 27001 compliant infrastructure**

## How Commercial Differs from DoW and FedRAMP

| Dimension | Commercial | FedRAMP | DoW |
|---|---|---|---|
| Compliance framework | SOC2 / ISO 27001 | FedRAMP Moderate | DoD RMF / ATO |
| ATO required | No | Varies by program | Yes |
| Network | Commercial internet | Commercial internet (with controls) | NIPRNET |
| CAC/PIV required | No | No | Required at IL5 |
| Deployment Passport | Not required | Not applicable | Required |
| Onboarding timeline | 7–30 days | 30–90 days | 60–120 days |
| Data classification | Commercial / unclassified | Unclassified federal | CUI / CDI |

<Callout type="note">
  Commercial deployments do not provide a path to DoD IL4/IL5 authorization on their own. If you anticipate needing IL4 or IL5 in the future, discuss your roadmap with your Second Front representative before committing to the commercial path.
</Callout>
