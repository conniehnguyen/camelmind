Follow these steps to get started with Game Warden.

<Steps>
  <Step step="1" title="Create your account">
    Navigate to the Game Warden portal and register with your government email.
  </Step>
  <Step step="2" title="Request access">
    Submit an access request for your organization. Allow 1–2 business days for approval.
  </Step>
  <Step step="3" title="Log in to App Central">
    Once approved, log in to App Central to begin onboarding your application.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="important">
  Your account must be linked to a verified organization before you can submit applications.
</Callout>
