This page walks through the process of creating a Game Warden account, registering your organization, and setting up your first Commercial deployment environment.

## Step 1: Register Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step step="1" title="Go to the Game Warden Portal">
    Navigate to [portal.gamewarden.io](https://portal.gamewarden.io) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step step="2" title="Enter Your Work Email">
    Use your company email address. Personal email addresses are not permitted.
  </Step>
  <Step step="3" title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Game Warden. Click the confirmation link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step step="4" title="Set Up MFA">
    Enroll in multi-factor authentication using a TOTP app or FIDO2 hardware key. MFA is required for all Game Warden accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Business Verification

After creating your account, you must verify your business identity before you can create or join an organization. Game Warden requires:

- A valid business domain matching your email address
- Your organization's legal name
- A brief description of your product and target use case

Business verification is reviewed by the Second Front onboarding team and typically completes within 1 business day.

<Callout type="note">
  If your organization is already registered in Game Warden, select **Request to Join an Organization** instead of creating a new one. Your Org Admin will need to approve your request.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Billing Setup

Commercial deployments are billed based on resource consumption (compute hours, storage, managed service usage). To activate billing:

1. Navigate to **Organization Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **Add Payment Method** and enter your payment information.
3. Select a billing plan. Contact your Second Front account representative if you require invoiced billing or a purchase order.

## Step 4: Invite Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step step="1" title="Navigate to Organization Settings">
    Go to **Organization Settings > Members** in the portal.
  </Step>
  <Step step="2" title="Invite a Member">
    Click **Invite Member**, enter the team member's work email, and select their role (Org Admin, Developer, or Read-Only).
  </Step>
  <Step step="3" title="Team Member Completes Registration">
    The invited team member receives an email invitation and must complete account registration and MFA enrollment before accessing the organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>
