Before you can submit an application on the FedRAMP path, you must complete account registration and organization setup in the Game Warden portal.

<Callout type="important">
  Your organization must be verified before you can submit applications for FedRAMP deployment. Verification typically takes 1–3 business days. Begin account setup early to avoid delays in your onboarding timeline.
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

Before starting, gather the following:
- An organizational email address (government contractor or company domain; personal email addresses are not accepted)
- Your organization's CAGE code (Commercial and Government Entity code)
- Your organization's UEI number
- Primary Point of Contact (POC) name, title, and phone number
- Name of the federal agency or program office you are contracting with

## Account Registration

<Steps>
  <Step step="1" title="Navigate to the Game Warden Portal">
    Go to [portal.gamewarden.io](https://portal.gamewarden.io) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step step="2" title="Enter Your Organizational Email">
    Enter your work email address. The domain must match your registered organization.
  </Step>
  <Step step="3" title="Verify Your Email">
    Open the verification email from Game Warden and click the confirmation link. The link expires after 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step step="4" title="Set Up Multi-Factor Authentication">
    After email verification, enroll in MFA using a TOTP authenticator app or FIDO2 hardware key. SMS-based MFA is not supported on the FedRAMP path.
  </Step>
  <Step step="5" title="Create or Join Your Organization">
    If your organization is not yet registered, select **Create Organization** and enter your organization details. If it already exists, select **Request to Join** and an Org Admin will approve your request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organization Setup

When creating a new organization, you will be asked for:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Organization Name | Legal name of your company or entity |
| CAGE Code | 5-character CAGE code from SAM.gov |
| UEI Number | Unique Entity Identifier from SAM.gov |
| Primary POC Name | Name of the main technical contact |
| Primary POC Title | Job title of the POC |
| Primary POC Phone | Direct phone number for the POC |
| Contracting Agency | The federal agency or DoD component you are contracting with |
| Contract Number | Your contract or task order number (if available) |

## Linking to a FedRAMP Workspace

After organization setup, select **FedRAMP** as your deployment path. This links your organization to Game Warden's FedRAMP-authorized environment. Your Org Admin will receive a confirmation notification when the workspace is provisioned (typically within 1 business day).

<Callout type="tip">
  If your organization needs access to both FedRAMP and DoW environments, contact your Second Front representative to set up multi-path access.
</Callout>

## Inviting Team Members

Once your organization is set up, the Org Admin can invite additional team members:

1. Navigate to **Organization Settings > Members**.
2. Click **Invite Member** and enter the team member's organizational email.
3. Select the appropriate role (Org Admin, Developer, or Read-Only).
4. Click **Send Invitation**. The invitee will receive an email to complete registration.
