This page explains how to submit an application for review.

<Callout type="warning">
  You must complete your organization profile before submitting an application.
</Callout>

## Steps to Submit

<Steps>
  <Step step="1" title="Open App Central">
    Navigate to App Central from the main dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step step="2" title="Create a new application">
    Click **New Application** and fill in the required fields.
  </Step>
  <Step step="3" title="Upload your Body of Evidence">
    Attach your BoE documentation package. See the BoE guide for required sections.
  </Step>
  <Step step="4" title="Submit for review">
    Click **Submit** to send your application to the review queue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
